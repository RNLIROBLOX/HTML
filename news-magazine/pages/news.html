CEO Reveals Latest Upgrade

On Wednesday 24th May 2017 the RNLI CEO Lukas_Smith tweeted "Glad to announce the arrival of a Tamar Class Lifeboat should be arriving in Southend-On-Sea this summer, ready for a busy summer!"

As seen below Lukas never revealed a time or an image but is only expecting a busy time this summer, does this mean a bigger upate?

The Tamar lifeboat is expected to be arriving in July but that's only a rumour going around head office, but there is one thing you can do! There is a competition to name the boat all you have to do is to tweet us what we should call it on twitter @rnli_roblox if you win the boat will be fitted with a name plate reading RNLB _____ (Your sugested name) and you will be taken for a tour round Southend-On-Sea on ROBLOX by land, sea and air!

The RNLI is due to be setting up a ROBLOX Stormforce magazine for supporters and will be putting cool stories, make your own kits and other stuff! We will also be releasing merchandise shortly!

Ever wanted to work for the RNLI>? Check if you're elligable now at the 'Applications' tab of this website, as always thank you!
