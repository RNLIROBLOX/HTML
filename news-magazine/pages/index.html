Essex Air Ambulance ROBLOX

Essex Air Ambulance is an emergency medical unit ran by Miniwader operating out of Southend Airport saving lives of many tourists and civilians in critical conditions across of Essex. Essex Air Ambulance ROBLOX works closely with Coastguard, RAF Search And Rescue, Essex Fire And Rescue, Lifeguards and the RNLI.

Essex Fire And Rescue »

Essex County Fire And Rescue Service ROBLOX

Essex County Fire And Rescue Service ROBLOX is ran by Techwizard789 and mainly operates at Southend Airport using Panthers but do rarely help with fires around the area ranging from crashed aircraft on beaches to cutting people out of cars on a RTC and work closely with other emergency services.


RAF Search And Rescue »

RAF Search And Rescue ROBLOX

Royal Air Force Search And Rescue ROBLOX work alongside coastguard and RNLI on airlifting people stranded/trapped to safety and issuing medical treatment. They also help locate missing person(s). No. 202 Squadron RAF is ran by DPM_Blinder and operates out of Southend Airport.

Royal National Lifeboat Institution ROBLOX

Royal National Lifeboat Institution ROBLOX is ran by Lukas_Smith and operates mainly at Southend-On-Sea. They mainly use Atlantic Class lifeboats and a hovercraft although they will be recieving a new Tamar Class lifeboat shortly.
