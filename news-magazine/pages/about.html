What is RNLI ROBLOX?

RNLI ROBLOX is an organisation that saves lives at sea on ROBLOX. The CEO Lukas_Smith set it up in the early summer 2017, with thanks to DesertCawood for our first beach of which we operated on. Also Dukeston_J who has been a Head Of Lifeguard since the start. There are also a few others who have helped from the start. DPM_Blinder (PlutoTheThird) who started off running 711 Royal Naval Air Support and who is now running RAF Search and Rescue, Techwizard789 and Treliux who helped develop Southend-On-Sea!

Where do RNLI ROBLOX operate?

RNLI ROBLOX is currently operating at Southend-On-Sea developed by CEO Lukas_Smith and Treliux. Southend is a seaside town with a small airport and a mile long pier!

What do RNLI ROBLOX use?

RNLI ROBLOX Lifeguards have equipment as listed; Surfboards, ATV's, 4x4s, Jet Ski and Inshore Rescue Boats! The RNLI use Inshore Rescue Hovercraft, B Class (Atlantic) and shortly a Tamar Class lifeboat. Crew members will shortly recieve cars to get to the station in swiftly.

What can I do at Southend-On-Sea?

You can swim, surf, Jet Ski, dive, kayak and get a tan. Maybe grab a refreshing ice cream and maybe a bag of chips and get a nice tan in the summer heat! Their will also be tour boats taking you for an eye catching tour of Southend by land and sea!

Am I safe at the beach?

Our lifeguards are trained to the best of their ability and know how to deal with cituations efficiently and effectively. Their should be flags up on the status of certain zones of water so make sure to obey them!

What happens if i'm stranded in the middle of the water?

If you are stranded the main thing to do is think about how to keep afloat if there is any debrit that floats near you try keep hold of it or even better get on it, if you have a lifejacket make sure to use it and most of all CALL 999 and our expert crews will get to you as quick as possible! If you're hungry or thirsty we have refreshments onboard. Worried you won't get found? Don't worry we have RAF Search And Rescue and HM Coastguard helicopters to help us out!
